Screen time log

Record approximate minutes per day for each family member. Data stays in this browser’s localStorage on this device — it does not sync to the cloud. Export a backup JSON occasionally. Compare totals to your policy limits as a family check-in, not a court verdict.

Family members

Give each person a name and pick their tier (used for Quota %). At least one person stays listed.

Live session timer

Pick who is on a device and how long they plan to use it, then Start. You can run several timers at once (one per person). Use Pause for breaks (elapsed time is added to that person’s today total when the box below is on). Resume continues that session. Stop ends it and adds any remaining rounded minutes. Warnings appear on each card before the planned time ends.

Warnings: ~5 min left (only if the plan is longer than 5 minutes), ~1 min left (if the plan is at least 2 minutes), ~30 sec left (if the plan is at least 45 seconds). This tab must stay open for the timer.

Log minutes for a day

Enter total screen minutes that day (all apps & games combined), per person. Saving overwrites that date if you log again.
